## üéØ CRITICAL REALITY CHECKS

### **What "Automated" Really Means:**

**NOT Automated (Manual Work Required):**
- Customer communication (questions, clarifications, revisions)
- Quality control (checking outputs before delivery)
- Platform management (responding to messages, handling disputes)
- Marketing (posting, engaging, building reputation)
- Learning curve (first few orders take 2-3x longer)

**Partially Automated:**
- Code generation (AI helps, but you customize)
- 3D model generation (AI creates, you verify and fix)
- Scraping scripts (template exists, customize per client)
- Dashboard building (base template, customize per client)

**Truly Automated (Set and Forget):**
- Recurring services after setup (AI influencer management)
- API subscriptions (once built, just runs)
- Very few services are actually this level

### **The 80/20 Rule:**
- 80% of time: Non-billable (marketing, support, admin)
- 20% of time: Billable (actual service delivery)

### **Platform Realities:**

**Fiverr:**
- Takes 20% commission
- You need 10+ reviews to rank well
- First 3 months: Very few orders (new seller penalty)
- Competition: 1,000+ sellers in same category
- Average order value: Lower than advertised

**Upwork:**
- Takes 20% commission (first $500 with client), then 10%
- Proposal acceptance rate: 5-10% for new sellers
- Need to spend 10-20 connects per proposal ($0.15 each)
- Competition: Offshore developers at $5-15/hr
